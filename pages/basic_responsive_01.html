Responsive Middle

This middle section expands and contracts with the viewport. Resize the window or rotate your phone to see it adapt.

No Page Scroll

The page height is locked to 100dvh (dynamic viewport height), so the footer always stays pinned to the bottom. The outer gap is controlled by --page-gap and includes iOS safe-area insets.

Tweak Sizes Quickly

  • Change --header-h and --footer-h to adjust bar heights.
  • Change --page-gap to grow/shrink the browser-edge gap.
  • Remove overflow:auto on main to forbid inner scrolling too.
