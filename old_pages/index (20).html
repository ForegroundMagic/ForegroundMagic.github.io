The 3001C is a soft, lightweight jersey tee designed to be a reliable everyday blank. The smooth surface and tight-knit jersey make it a strong choice for print work, keeping details crisp and consistent.

Typical construction highlights include side seams (for a more flattering, consistent drape) and shoulder-to-shoulder taping (to help maintain shape). Solid colorways are commonly listed as 100% Airlume combed and ring-spun cotton, while heather/blend variants may include polyester.
